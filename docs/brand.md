# Brand foundation for unyt.shop

## Positioning
`unyt.shop` is the premium public face of the UNYT ecosystem: a commerce and utility platform where users can add UNYTs, spend them across a growing network of products and services, and access Arbitrum-based utility where available.

## Brand promise
One balance across every product.

## Tone
- Premium
- Calm
- Exact
- Useful
- Global
- Trustworthy

## What the brand must not feel like
- A speculative token landing page
- A meme coin site
- A trading dashboard
- A generic startup template
- A noisy Web3 homepage

## Design references
Aim for the clarity of Stripe, the polish of Apple, and the product confidence of a top-tier fintech brand.

## Keywords to use
- balance
- utility
- spend
- pay
- supported
- ecosystem
- wallet
- secure
- clear
- trusted

## Keywords to avoid
- invest
- moon
- alpha
- pump
- passive income
- upside
- guaranteed
- early buyers

## Core headline system
- Homepage hero eyebrow: ONE BALANCE. MANY PRODUCTS.
- Homepage hero H1: Spend UNYTs across the entire ecosystem.
- Support line: One balance across every product.

## Audience
1. Existing ecosystem users who need a simpler way to pay across products.
2. New users who want a premium, mobile-first wallet and checkout flow.
3. Partners evaluating whether to accept UNYTs.

## Success criteria
A sophisticated user should instantly understand:
1. What UNYTs are used for.
2. Where they can be spent.
3. Why the experience is easier than fragmented product payments.
4. That this is a real utility network, not a speculative promotion.
